instructions for reimbursing expenses for SOSP2013

Note (posted 11/7/13): these instructions are for reimbursements to organizing committee members. Reimbursements for student travel scholarships will use a separate system. Detailed instructions for how to claim reimbursement for student travel scholarships are not available yet, but will be issued as soon as possible.
  1. Fill out the ACM expense template. Notes:
    • Ignore the fact that the title of the form says "volunteer travel." This is the correct form for other types of expenses too.
    • In the "charge to cost center" cell, put "SIGOPS--SOSP2013."
    • In the "committee position" cell, describe your role within the conference, whether or not you are actually on the organizing committee.
    • If this isn't a travel claim, put "none" in the "destination" cell.
  2. Send an electronic copy of the expense template together with electronic copies of original receipts for all expenses to both of the following people:
    • the conference treasurer, John MacCormick (jmac@dickinson.edu)
    • the ACM Program Coordinator for Conference Operations, April Mosqus (mosqus@hq.acm.org)
  3. [Optional alternative] If you prefer to send hard copies of the expense template and receipts by physical mail, you may do this instead of sending electronic copies by e-mail. In this case, send a hard copy of the expense template together with original receipts for all expenses by physical mail to both

    Carlisle, PA 17013

  4. ACM will send you a check by physical mail.

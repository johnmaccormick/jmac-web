Exams

Exam 1

Exam 1 will examine material up to and including class 12. More precisely, the exam covers material up to and including Example 4.6 on page 114 of the textbook, but excluding the pumping lemma itself. The exam is open-note: you may use any written or printed materials. You may also use JFLAP. You may also use a web browser to access the class web pages and the class resources on Moodle. No other electronic devices or online resources may be used. A practice exam, with solutions, is provided on Moodle. Most questions will be similar to a homework, EZquiz or practice exam question. You will have 75 minutes to complete the exam.

Final Exam

The final exam will examine all material taught in the course (except where explicitly stated as non-examinable). The exam is open-note: you may use any written or printed materials. You may also use JFLAP. You may also use a web browser to access the class web pages and the class resources on Moodle. No other electronic devices or online resources may be used. A practice exam, with solutions, is provided on Moodle. Most questions will be similar to a homework, EZquiz or practice exam question. You will have 3 hours to complete the exam.

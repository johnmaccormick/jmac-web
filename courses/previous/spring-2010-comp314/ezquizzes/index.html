EZquizzes

Note that EZquizzes are cumulative. The documents below list the potential questions that will be added for each EZquiz. For example, EZquiz 3 will consist of a selection of questions from the first three documents below. Also, note that you may be required to explain how you obtained the answer to any question, clearly describing each step in the algorithm you used. Therefore, it is not sufficient to merely memorize the answer.

Starting with EZquiz 4, EZquizzes will not be cumulative. [added 3/24/2010]

Beat Ninja
Description:

A classic beat game where you slice and dice music to the beat of the music!
Controls:
  • LEFT: Slice fruits coming from the left
  • RIGHT: Slice fruits coming from the right
  • SPACE: Slice fruits coming from above
Technology Used:
  • Visual Studio C++
  • SFML
  • Audacity Free Audio Editor and Recorder
Music:

Miche - Hungarian Dance Techno Remix with some slight modifications.

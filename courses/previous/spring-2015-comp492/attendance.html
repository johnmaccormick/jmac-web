Attendance policy

As of 3/9/15, the senior seminar will adopt the following attendance policy. An unexcused absence from class will reduce your final grade by 1%. Arriving for class more than five minutes late will reduce your final grade by 0.5%. An absence is "excused" if I give you permission to miss class in advance. In extremely rare situations (e.g. you broke your leg on the way to class), an absence could be excused after class.

Homework Assignments

Use the links above to answer the following questions.

  1. The history of computing is often broken into generations with the generations delineated by a change in technology. What technologies defined the mechanical era and each of the first four generations of computers?
  2. In what way are electromechanical relays, vacuum tubes and transistors similar? In what ways are they different?
  3. What does the acronym VLSI stand for? Why was this technological improvement important?
  4. To which generation of computers did the CRAY 2 belong? Why did it belong to this generation?
  5. Name three important figures in the history of computing and briefly describe their contribution.

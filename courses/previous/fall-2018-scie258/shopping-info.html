Grocery and shopping information

For grocery and other shopping, here are a few good options relative to your accommodation at the University Village:
  • Closest: The Co-op at Fiveways. There is also a Tesco Metro here, but I think the Co-op may be a little bigger and better. Almost equally close is the on-campus grocery store called ShopSU.
  • A little further away, but bigger and better: The Co-op at the junction of Earlham and Recreation Roads. Not too far to walk and also directly on the number 26 bus line.
  • Biggest but furthest: The Morrisons in the Riverside Retail Park, right at the end of the number 25 bus line. Takes a while to get there but the bus drops you right outside a big store that has everything, a bit like Walmart.
  • Cheapest: The discount grocery store ALDI is a good option for low-cost groceries. It's a 20 minute walk from the Village and not easily accessible by bus from the Village.
  • Of course there are many excellent stores in the city center, which you can reach on the number 25 or number 26 bus lines. The best-known department stores are: Jarrolds (an independent Norwich store), Debenhams (probably the cheapest), Marks and Spencer, John Lewis (a little more upscale but still good value).

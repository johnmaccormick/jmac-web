COMP 131 - Web-CAT Tips

  • To change your password: Log into Web-CAT. Select Home and then My Profile at the top of the screen. You will get a dialog box where you can enter (and confirm) a new password.


  • To submit a BlueJ project:
    • In BlueJ, select Tools > Submit from the menubar.
    • In the dialog box that appears, make sure the correct assignment is selected. (You may need to click Browse, then double-click on the COSCI folder, and then select the correct assignment.)
    • Press Submit.
    • Another dialog box will appear for you to enter your username and password.
    • Finally, you should get a link to click on to view your results in a web browser.


  • To view your grades:
    • Log onto the Web-CAT system.
    • Click "Results" and then "Most recent" in the upper-right corner.
    • This will show you the most recent submission of the most recent assignment you worked on. If this is not the assignment you want to view, click "Change Assignment" in the upper-right corner.
    • In the dialog window that appears, check the "closed" checkbox near the bottom (this allows you to choose assignments that are already closed as well as those that are still open for submission). Then select the assignment you are interested in, and click OK.
